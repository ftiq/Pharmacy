ODOO POS Pharmacy Management

Advanced Pharmacy Management in Odoo POS!

POS Pharmacy Management expands the horizon of Odoo POS. The module makes the POS compatible so it can efficiently manage a pharmacy. The Odoo app adds a Pharmacy menu to the Odoo POS menus. From the Pharmacy menu, you can add doctors, medicine products, manufacturers, diseases, salts, usage, warnings, etc. The pharmacy management module is the complete solution for a pharmacy.

Detailed Features List

Below is the list of features Odoo POS Pharmacy Management

Create Medicine Products in the Odoo Backend

  • The Odoo app allows you to create medicine (products) from the backend.
  • Add details like salt composition, salt alternate, medicine use, side effects, etc.
  • You can also manage multiple UOM (Units of Measure) for a medicine.

Using Advance Search in the Odoo POS

  • Use the advanced search icon or menu option from the menu.
  • Enable/disable the advanced search with a click from the Odoo backend.
  • It helps filter out products quickly according to the selected criteria.

Enhanced Odoo POS Interface

  • Added a new interface for the Odoo POS front end to ease navigation.
  • The new interface helps sort and filter out products easily and smoothen the process.

Configuring various Elements from the Odoo Backend

  • You can enable or disable multiple settings from the Odoo backend.
  • You can add or remove different elements related to the pharmacy from the setting menu.

Adding Medicine Details from the Odoo Backend

  • You can add various details related to medicine.
  • Add manufacturers, diseases, salts, usage, side effects, chemical class, and more.

Display Medicine Details

  • The Odoo app adds a medicine information icon over the product image.
  • The icon opens a popup that shows every related information related to the medicine.

Adding a Secondary Screen in Odoo POS

  • The POS Pharmacy Management module allows you to add and show products on the secondary customer screen.

Add Quickpay Payment Option

  • The Odoo app allows you to add the Quickpay option for a smooth payment process.

Check and locate Alternate Medicine

  • The module also shows alternate medicine and their links in the medicine information popup.

Configuring the Default Product View

  • You can choose between grid and list view for your default Odoo POS product display.

Product Images in Orderline

  • If configured from the backend, the Odoo app shows product images in the orderline.

Specify Details to Show in Product Information Popup

  • You can select the details to show the medicine information popup while configuring the module from the backend.

Empty the Cart at Once in Odoo POS

  • The module allows deleting/emptying the entire cart with just a click.
  • Or you can use the cancel icon to remove individual product units.

Configure the POS Color

  • The module allows you to select the desired color from the palette.
  • You can choose the desired color that matches your store theme.

Adding Doctors to the Order

  • You can add a doctor to the order identical to adding a customer.
  • The Order receipt will show the doctor's name in the Odoo backend.

Adding Product to the Cart

  • You can add medicine to the cart with a drag-and-drop feature.
Why Manage Your Pharmacy in Odoo POS?
The Pharmacy Management system market size was $15.66 billion in 2020. It is expected to grow at an annual CAGR of 14.6%, and at this rate, it will reach $35.49 billion by 2026. Hence, the chances for growth and business expansion are plenty.
Odoo POS streamlines online and offline store management. Also, this pharmacy management module allows you to efficiently manage every pharmacy operation. It has advanced search, the option to add a doctor, toggle view, drag and drop, Quickpay, and many more.
The module offers a better interface, more icon options, and offer quick and hassle-free navigation.
What POS Pharmacy Management is Capable of?
Odoo POS Pharmacy Management is capable of efficiently managing your pharmacy. You can manage your inventory, stock inflow or outflow, and add doctors, manufacturers, composition salts, medicines, usage, side effects, chemical class, and various other details.
It offers an improved interface with quick navigation icons, advanced search to filter out medicine quickly, a drag and drop feature to add products, add loyalty points, a secondary customer screen, clear or delete the cart, medicine details, alternates, and much more.
Benefits of Managing Pharmacy in POS
The pharmacy management module offers the following benefits.
  • It helps streamline the workflow, which includes collecting prescriptions, inventory management, and sales. It automates inventory tracking, repetitive tasks, etc.
  • The system secures patients' details like history, medication profile, etc. It helps in accessing relevant information quickly to ensure accurate dispensing.
  • The medicine information popup shows all the medicine-related details, which include side effects, dosage, safety tips, and medicine alternatives.
  • POS system delivers a centralized database for all relevant details, which enables you to provide personalized service to the customers. Also, it reduces the order processing time to enhance the overall customer experience.
  • In addition, it offers rich reporting insights into sales, inventory, etc. It helps identify growth opportunities, optimize inventory, and form business decisions.
